Why Choose Coco's?

Our chocolates are expertly made with all natural ingredients to ensure quality. Coco's Chocolatiers have been in business for 50 years and are world renowned for our tasty recipes. We have a wide variety of chocolates including dark, milk and white chocolate. When placing an order with us, you have the choice to choose from our preset options or to customize your own chocolate bar.

What are our main ingredients?

  1. Milk
  2. Cocoa
  3. Sugar
  4. Vanilla
  5. Cocoa Butter
  6. Coconut Oil

Who are we?

We are sisters who both love to make (and eat) chocolate. Holly is the true chocolatier between the two of us, whereas Jenn spends the majority of her time on marketing. We have been running this business for 10 years and expect to be running it for many years to come.
